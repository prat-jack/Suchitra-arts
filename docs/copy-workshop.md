# Copy workshop — v1 (three voices)

Every sentence on the site is currently a build-time placeholder. This doc
proposes full site copy in three voices — same structure everywhere so lines
can be mixed. **REC** marks the recommended cut (assembled at the bottom).
Rendered review page: see the "Suchitra Arts — Copy Workshop" artifact.

## The voices

- **A — The Craftsman.** Short declaratives, materials named, zero fluff.
  *"Measured twice. Built once. Lit before handover."*
- **B — The Neighbour.** Warm, first-person plural, Bengaluru-rooted.
  *"Drop by — your sign's probably half-built on the bench."*
- **C — The Gallerist.** Sparse, editorial, lets the visuals talk.
  *"Designed flat. Built dimensional. Lit by hand."*

## How we work

*(Recommendation: trust section — Voice B carries the steps.)*

**Intro**
- A **REC** *(current)*: One workshop, one crew, no hand-offs. The person who
  measures your wall is the person who bolts the sign to it.
- B: You'll deal with the same people from first site visit to switch-on.
  That's the point of a workshop, not an agency.
- C: Four steps. No middlemen.

**01 Measure**
- A *(current)*: We start at your shopfront, not a moodboard — facade
  dimensions, mounting points, power routing, sight lines from the street.
- B **REC**: First, we come to you. Ten minutes at the shop tells us more
  than ten emails: where the light falls, where the eyes go, where the wire
  runs.
- C: Site first. The street decides the sign.

**02 Design & proof**
- A *(current)*: Your artwork becomes a scale fabrication drawing —
  materials, lighting and fixings specified, signed off before anything is cut.
- B **REC**: We turn your logo into a drawing you can hold — every material
  named, every cost visible. You approve it before a single sheet is cut.
- C: A drawing you sign. Then we cut.

**03 Fabricate**
- A **REC** *(current)*: Cut, bent, welded and wired in our BTM Layout
  workshop — by the same hands that will install it.
- B: Built here, not outsourced. Want to see your sign half-made? Drop by —
  customers do.
- C: In-house, end to end.

**04 Install & light up**
- A *(current)*: Mounted, cabled, tested — and switched on with you watching.
  We don't leave until it glows.
- B **REC**: Switch-on is the best part of this job. We stay until it's
  glowing and you've taken the photo.
- C: We leave when it glows.
